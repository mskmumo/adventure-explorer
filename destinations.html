Popular Safari Destinations

Masai Mara (Kenya)

Duration: 5-7 Days

Group Tours Available

Witness the Great Migration and engage with Maasai culture.

Featured Activities: Game drives, photography, cultural visits.

Serengeti (Tanzania)

Duration: 6-8 Days

Private Tours Available

Explore the vast plains and rich wildlife, home to the Big Five.

Featured Activities: Safari tours, hot air balloon rides, wildlife photography.

Kruger National Park (South Africa)

Duration: 7-10 Days

Best for both family and solo travelers.

One of the largest game reserves, known for diverse wildlife.

Featured Activities: Safari drives, bird watching, camping.

Chobe National Park (Botswana)

Duration: 4-6 Days

Ideal for short, action-packed safaris.

Known for large herds of elephants and stunning sunsets over the river.

Featured Activities: Boat cruises, wildlife spotting, guided tours.
